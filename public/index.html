Workbook Notes

This page mirrors the spreadsheet logic as closely as practical in browser code. It remains a transparent engineering aid rather than a stamped design package.

Ported calculations

  • Geometry derivations for r, b_e, B_tot, H, Z', A_sf, and Z_plastic.
  • BTH-1 allowable stresses and safety factors used by the workbook.
  • FM1 through FM6 allowable load formulas and governing-capacity summary.

Important notes

  • FM4 uses the workbook's 0.9 loose-pin bearing coefficient from the note set.
  • FM2 limits the effective rupture width to min(b_e, 2t + 0.63 in).
  • FM6 follows the workbook's not-checked behavior when the out-of-plane angle is 0 degrees.
