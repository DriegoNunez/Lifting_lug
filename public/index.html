Excel source-of-truth implementation

1. Material Properties

Yellow cells are editable inputs. Read-only cells mirror workbook-derived values.

2. Lug Section Geometry

The geometry table keeps the workbook input rows and helper-cell sequence `r`, `b_e`, `B_tot`, and `H`.

3. Loading Information

Workbook demand inputs plus the ASME B30 shear-plane reduction term `Z'`.

4. Allowable Stresses & Safety Factors

ASME BTH-1 allowables and the workbook safety-factor treatment, including the blank rupture omega column.

5. Failure Mode Capacities

Each mode keeps its own helper row, formula text, capacity, workbook figure region, and governing flag.

6. Design Summary

The summary mirrors the workbook review table and governing-capacity row.

7. Notes

The workbook notes are preserved verbatim in intent and order.
